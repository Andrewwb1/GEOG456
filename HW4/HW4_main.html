Project Data Storage

US Census Data was extracted from IPUMS for the period 1850-2021. Birthplace data was collected at the household level, then aggregated to the state level. Data was processed in R. I limited the data to the South Atlantic Division, as defined by the Census. Data citation: Steven Ruggles, Sarah Flood, Matthew Sobek, Daniel Backman, Annie Chen, Grace Cooper, Stephanie Richards, Renae Rodgers, and Megan Schouweiler. IPUMS USA: Version 15.0 [dataset]. Minneapolis, MN: IPUMS, 2024. https://doi.org/10.18128/D010.V15.0
